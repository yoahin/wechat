know of v.s. know about

know about sth/sb = have heard many times about this thing or person, and know some details about it/him/her;
know of sth/sb = have merely heard of sth/sb, and that's all

依照瞭解程度幽深的到淺：know about > know of。不過現在這種區別再被逐漸淡化，know of 和 know about 不少字典都不做區分了。
